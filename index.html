Hobbies

Music

Music is a way to unwind while working, detaching from the real world in order to either empower my creative self or to be able to imagine and better understand the subtleties of the work i'm undergoing.

This combined with learning an instrument, the guitar, further enhances my perception of the world.

Reading

Reading is a way to soothe my curious, eager to explore new worlds self, a way to understand the past and how it may affect the future. Seeing the world as it was, is and will be through another person's eyes can be a way to understand it better than having only your own perception.

Gaming

Gaming is the reason i started to grow an interest in the tech world. Seeing all the interesting, amazing things one could do with a computer, all the breathtaking projects and games that could be developed made me interested in programming and technologies that could lead me to create such a project of my own.

This passion quickly grew bigger than just the gaming domain and into the tech world.
